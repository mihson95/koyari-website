encouraging comments from our discerning guests who booked their stay through AirBnB:
Rima (Kolhapur) We absolutely enjoyed our stay. Great food, amazing hospitality, sparkling clean house. Mr. Phadke was continuously in touch. Mr.Kadam was available on property 24*7 for us, and that was the best part of our stay.

Dashmit (Pune) We found staying at Koyari with family a very unique experience, a hundred times better than a hotel/resort stay. Squeaky clean rooms and toilets and very nicely decorated with fresh flowers, traditional rangoli and incense sticks. All amenities like bedsheets, pillows, towels etc were provided and were extremely clean and fresh. Food prepared by local village neighbors was excellent and authentic. Anirudha was a great host, although not physically present, constantly in touch throughout our stay ensuring all arrangements were proper. Although our stay was short we enjoyed being in the lap of nature. Would surely visit again. Just one suggestion though, to possibly organize some farm activity which kids can participate in.

Shahbaz (Mumbai) Property looks amazingly new despite being 3-4 years old. It is maintained well by caretaker Mr. Kadam. He is a generous person with a big heart who can go above and beyond for customer satisfaction. Food was delicious and property location is right in the middle of farms and village. Perfect location to find peace and serenity. Definitely a 5-star rating for hospitality, food and property maintenance. Must visit.
