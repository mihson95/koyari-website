House Rules

Check-in window: 12 pm to 8 pm

Check-out time: 10 am or earlier
  • Smoking inside the house is strictly prohibited.
  • No pets allowed inside the house.
  • Consumption of Alcohol is not permitted, however special permission can be obtained from the Owner prior to confirming a booking.
  • No loud music/parties or loud conversations outdoors after 10 pm to maintain the tranquility of the village and avoid disturbing the neighbors.
  • Kitchen is not open for general guest use, only Mothers with infants are allowed entry to the Kitchen, for warming milk, or preparing any kids meals. Please remember to carry the ingredients required for preparing any kids meals.
  • A Fridge is available in the Kitchen for Guest usage, however no raw meat or fish shall be allowed to be stored. Other kitchen equipment is not available for Guest use.
  • Guests are allowed to get outside food but the staff will not be able to warm the food brought from outside or serve it or provide cutlery.
