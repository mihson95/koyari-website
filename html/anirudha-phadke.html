Anirudha Phadke

Greetings!! to friends, well-wishers and guests who will be staying with us. I would like to take this opportunity to introduce myself and the whole concept of Koyari Vacation Home. I am an Architect by profession and a nature enthusiast and organic farmer by passion. I enjoy sketching, photography, and wildlife conservation as creative hobbies.

I acquired this property back in 2017 after a long and arduous search. The property consisted of a 100-year old dilapidated house and surrounding 2-acre farmland which was in a state of neglect. I was faced with the immediate and daunting task of preserving what is already there as heritage and rebuilding something new and exciting to be used by my family as a holiday home. I then thought of opening our family holiday home and sharing it with discerning guests who want that authentic Konkani holiday.

Using my Architectural skills, and sustainable methods of restoration and construction I have carefully restored this property by retaining most of the original character of a traditional Konkani house and adding modern day amenities which make the stay here a delightful experience.

I take great pleasure in curating that perfect holiday for my guests staying at Koyari. I am sure you will have a truly memorable stay at Koyari and will have many wonderful stories to share with your friends and relatives.
