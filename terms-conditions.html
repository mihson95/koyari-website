Koyari Vacation Home

Terms & Conditions

Check in time for Koyari Vacation Home is 12 noon and beyond, check out time is on or before 10 am.
No pets are allowed on the property. If Guests do arrive with pets, they are strongly advised to tie the pets in the outside Verandah (Padavi) and under no circumstances take the pets inside the house.
Smoking and consumption of alcohol is strictly prohibited inside the premises.
Pure Vegetarian Meals and Breakfast are outsourced to friendly neighbors, all requests for meals must be confirmed at the time of check-in and any specific requests with regards to spicy/salty foods and any specific food allergies should be notified to the Caretaker while placing the food order. The Owners are not liable for any allergy or ailments arising from the food. The total bill for the food order should be settled in cash at the time of check-out. Guests are permitted to get outside food but are advised against requesting our staff to serve or re-heat the food brought from outside as this will not be possible. Also Guests are advised to get their own disposable plates and cutlery as we will not provide kitchen utensils and plates for serving outside food.
Guests are to note that this property being the Vacation Home of the Owner is frequently visited and has many decorative items, cutlery, furniture and electronics. Any damage to any items inside the house and on the property will be billed to the Guest and an appropriate amount will be deducted from the security deposit.
Although we have taken all due care to ensure safety of guests and their wellbeing, the Owners of Koyari Vacation Home are not liable for any bodily injury or death due to activities on these premises. Guests are advised not to leave children unattended and ensure that handicapped persons and elderly persons are always accompanied.
Guests are advised to take care of their personal belongings. Owners of Koyari Vacation Home are not liable for any loss or damage to personal belongings of Guests.
